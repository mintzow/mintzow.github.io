      Manhunt in alabama



A Blue Alert has been issued for Austin Patrick Hall, the man suspected of shooting two Bibb County Sheriff's Office deputies Wednesday afternoon.

Hall is considered a serious threat to the public. The 26-year-old is described as being a white male with brown hair and hazel eyes. He stands 5 feet 9 inches tall and weighs approximately 169 pounds.

If you see something, call 205-926-3129, *HP (*47), or 911. Please include as much information as possible, especially descriptions and travel directions.


According to Bibb County District Attorney Michael Jackson, the deputies were shot on Highway 25.

During the pursuit of a stolen vehicle, the shooting occurred near the Cahaba River Wildlife Management Area.
